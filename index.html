Electric Scooters: A Modern Way to Travel

Date: 2026-06-15 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Electric scooters

这篇课文介绍了电动滑板车的使用方法和安全注意事项。

Electric scooters are small and light vehicles. They run on batteries, not gasoline. Many people ride them to go to work, school, or the store. They are cheap and fast. You can park them almost anywhere. They are good for the environment because they produce no pollution. Riding a scooter is easy and fun.

电动滑板车是小型轻便的交通工具。它们靠电池运行，不用汽油。许多人骑它们去上班、上学或去商店。它们便宜又快。你几乎可以停在任何地方。它们对环境友好，因为不产生污染。骑滑板车简单又有趣。

If you want to rent a scooter, use a phone app. First, find a scooter near you on the map. Scan the QR code to unlock it. You pay by the minute. When you finish, park it in a safe place. Some people buy their own scooter. They charge it at home. Rental scooters are very popular in big cities.

如果你想租滑板车，使用手机应用。首先，在地图上找到附近的滑板车。扫描二维码解锁。按分钟付费。结束后，停在安全的地方。有些人购买自己的滑板车。他们在家充电。租赁滑板车在大城市非常流行。

Safety is very important when riding a scooter. Always wear a helmet. Check the scooter before you ride. Make sure the brakes and tires work well. Do not ride on sidewalks. Do not use your phone while riding. Watch for cars and people. Follow traffic lights. If you follow these tips, you will be safe and have fun.

骑滑板车时安全非常重要。始终戴头盔。骑行前检查滑板车。确保刹车和轮胎工作正常。不要在人行道上骑行。骑行时不要用手机。注意汽车和行人。遵守交通信号灯。如果你遵循这些建议，你会安全又开心。
