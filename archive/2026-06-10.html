World Environment Day

Date: 2026-06-10 | Level: Beginner | Topic: World Environment Day and simple eco-friendly actions

这是一个关于世界环境日的简单英语课程。你将学到一些环保行动和常用单词。

Last week, on June 5, people around the world celebrated World Environment Day. This special day reminds us to take care of our planet. Many cities hold events to clean parks and plant trees.

上周，也就是6月5日，世界各地的人们庆祝了世界环境日。这个特殊的日子提醒我们要爱护我们的星球。许多城市举办活动来清洁公园和植树。

You can help the environment every day. For example, turn off lights when you leave a room. Use less water when you brush your teeth. Walk or ride a bike instead of taking a car. Small actions make a big difference.

你可以每天帮助环境。例如，离开房间时关灯。刷牙时少用水。步行或骑自行车代替开车。小小的行动带来很大的改变。

When we all work together, we can protect the Earth for the future. Let&#39;s start today. Every person can do something good for our world.

当我们大家一起努力，我们就能保护地球的未来。让我们从今天开始。每个人都可以为我们的世界做些好事。
