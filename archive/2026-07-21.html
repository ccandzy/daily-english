The Rise of Electric Vehicles

Date: 2026-07-21 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Electric vehicles

本课介绍电动汽车的普及、优缺点和未来趋势，帮助你学习相关词汇和表达。

Electric vehicles, or EVs, are becoming more popular around the world. Many people are choosing them because they are better for the environment. They produce less pollution than cars that use gasoline. They also use less energy.

电动汽车在世界各地越来越受欢迎。许多人选择它们，因为它们对环境更友好。它们比使用汽油的汽车产生的污染更少，而且能耗也更低。

EVs are cheaper to run because electricity costs less than fuel. However, they still have some problems. For example, the batteries do not last very long, and charging stations are not everywhere yet. Another issue is that EVs are often more expensive to buy.

电动汽车运行成本更低，因为电比燃料便宜。然而，它们仍然存在一些问题。例如，电池寿命不长，充电站也还没有普及。另一个问题是电动汽车的购买价格通常更高。

Many countries are working to build more charging stations. They also want to make batteries that last longer. As technology improves, prices are coming down. In the future, EVs may become the main type of car on the road.

许多国家正在努力建设更多的充电站。他们还希望制造更耐用的电池。随着技术的进步，价格正在下降。未来，电动汽车可能成为道路上的主要车型。
