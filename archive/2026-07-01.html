World Environment Day

Date: 2026-07-01 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Environmental protection

学习关于世界环境日的简单英语，了解环保的重要性。

June 5 is World Environment Day. On this special day, people in many countries do things to protect the Earth. They plant trees in parks, clean up beaches, and save water at home. You can also help! For example, you can turn off lights when you leave a room and use less plastic bags.

6月5日是世界环境日。在这个特殊的日子里，许多国家的人们做些事情来保护地球。他们在公园里植树，清理海滩，在家里节约用水。你也可以帮忙！例如，离开房间时关灯，少用塑料袋。

Taking care of the environment is very important for everyone. Clean air and clean water keep us healthy. When we protect nature, we also protect ourselves. Even small actions, like picking up trash, can make a big difference.

保护环境对每个人都很重要。干净的空气和干净的水让我们保持健康。当我们保护自然时，我们也在保护自己。即使是像捡垃圾这样的小行动也能产生很大的影响。

Today is a good day to start! Use a reusable water bottle instead of buying plastic ones. Ride a bike or walk for short trips. Every small step helps our planet. Together, we can make the world cleaner and greener.

今天是一个开始的好日子！使用可重复使用的水瓶，而不是购买塑料瓶。短途旅行时骑自行车或步行。每一个小步骤都有助于我们的星球。团结起来，我们可以让世界更干净、更环保。
