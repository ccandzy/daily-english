World Oceans Day

Date: 2026-06-22 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Environment and ocean protection

这篇课文介绍了世界海洋日以及保护海洋的重要性，适合初学者学习简单英语表达。

World Oceans Day is on June 8 every year. This special day was started by the United Nations. It helps people learn about the ocean and why it is important. The ocean covers more than 70% of our planet. It gives us food, oxygen, and many other things. We need to take care of the ocean.

世界海洋日是每年的6月8日。这个特殊的日子由联合国发起，帮助人们了解海洋及其重要性。海洋覆盖了地球70%以上的面积。它提供食物、氧气和许多其他东西。我们需要保护海洋。

The ocean is home to many animals like fish, whales, dolphins, and coral. It also helps control the weather and climate. But today, the ocean faces many problems. Pollution from plastic and chemicals harms the water and the animals. Too much plastic waste is in the ocean. This is very bad for sea life and even for humans.

海洋是许多动物的家园，比如鱼、鲸鱼、海豚和珊瑚。它也有助于控制天气和气候。但如今，海洋面临许多问题。塑料和化学污染危害了水和动物。海洋中塑料垃圾太多了。这对海洋生物甚至人类都很糟糕。

There are simple things we can do to help. We can use less plastic in our daily life. For example, bring a reusable water bottle instead of buying plastic bottles. We can also join clean-up activities on the beach. Another idea is to tell friends and family about ocean protection. Every small action helps. Together we can make a difference. Let&#39;s protect our beautiful ocean.

我们可以做一些简单的事情来帮忙。日常生活中少用塑料。例如，带可重复使用的水瓶代替买塑料瓶。还可以参加海滩清洁活动。另一个想法是告诉朋友和家人保护海洋。每个小行动都有帮助。一起努力，我们可以做出改变。让我们保护美丽的海洋。
