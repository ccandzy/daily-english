Sun Power for a Clean Future

Date: 2026-06-22 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Solar energy and its benefits

这个课程介绍太阳能如何帮助我们获得清洁能源。

Many people use solar energy. Solar panels catch sunlight and make electricity. This is good for the Earth.

很多人使用太阳能。太阳能板捕捉阳光并发电。这对地球有好处。

Solar energy is clean and cheap. It does not make pollution. More and more homes use solar power every year.

太阳能清洁又便宜。它不产生污染。越来越多的家庭每年使用太阳能。

You can see solar panels on roofs. They work on sunny days. Even on cloudy days, they can make some energy.

你可以在屋顶上看到太阳能板。它们在晴天工作。即使在阴天，它们也能产生一些能量。
