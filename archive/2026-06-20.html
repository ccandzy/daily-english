Summer Reading Challenge

Date: 2026-06-20 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Many people start a reading challenge in summer to learn and relax. This lesson introduces the idea and some useful words.

夏天来了，很多人喜欢读书。这个课程帮你学习关于夏天阅读挑战的简单英语。

Summer is a great time to read. Many people join a reading challenge. They try to finish one or two books each month. It is fun and helps you learn new things. You can read alone or with friends.

夏天是读书的好时候。很多人参加阅读挑战。他们试着每个月读完一两本书。这很有趣，还能帮你学到新东西。你可以自己读，也可以和朋友一起读。

First, choose a book that you like. It can be a story, a science book, or a book about travel. Then, find a quiet place to read every day. You can read for 20 minutes before bed. Little by little, you will finish the book.

首先，选一本你喜欢的书。可以是故事书、科学书，或者讲旅行的书。然后，每天找一个安静的地方读书。你可以在睡前读20分钟。一点点地，你就会读完那本书。

When you finish a book, write a short note about it. Tell your friends what you learned. You can also keep a list of all the books you read. This summer, try a reading challenge. It will make your vacation happy and smart!

当你读完一本书，写一点关于它的笔记。告诉朋友们你学到了什么。你也可以把你读过的书列一个清单。这个夏天，试试阅读挑战吧。它会让你的假期又快乐又聪明！
