Summer Fun with Friends

Date: 2026-06-26 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Staying active and enjoying summer safely

一篇关于夏季乐趣的简单英文短文，附中文翻译，适合初学者学习。

Summer is here. The weather is very hot and sunny. Many people like to go swimming in a pool or at the beach. Swimming is a good exercise and it is fun. You can play with your friends in the water. You can also learn to swim or just splash around. Swimming makes you feel cool on a hot day.

夏天来了。天气非常炎热晴朗。许多人喜欢去游泳池或海滩游泳。游泳是一项很好的运动，也很有趣。你可以和朋友在水里玩耍。你也可以学游泳或者只是泼水玩。游泳让你在炎热的日子里感到凉爽。

After swimming, you can eat ice cream. Ice cream comes in many flavors like chocolate, strawberry, and vanilla. It is cold and sweet. You can also play games outside. For example, you can play football, basketball, or frisbee. These games are very fun and help you stay active. Eating ice cream with friends is a happy time.

游泳后，你可以吃冰淇淋。冰淇淋有很多口味，比如巧克力、草莓和香草。它又冷又甜。你也可以在外面玩游戏。例如，你可以踢足球、打篮球或玩飞盘。这些游戏很有趣，还能让你保持活力。和朋友一起吃冰淇淋是快乐的时光。

But you must be careful in the sun. The sun can burn your skin. Wear a hat and sunglasses. Use sunscreen to protect your skin. Drink a lot of water to stay hydrated. It is also good to take breaks in the shade. If you feel tired, rest for a while. This helps you enjoy summer without getting sick. Have a great summer!

但在太阳下你必须小心。太阳会晒伤你的皮肤。戴上帽子和太阳镜。使用防晒霜保护皮肤。多喝水保持水分。在阴凉处休息也很好。如果感到累了，就休息一会儿。这能帮助你享受夏天而不生病。祝夏天愉快！
