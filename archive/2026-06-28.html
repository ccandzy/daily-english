Summer Fruits

Date: 2026-06-28 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Summer Fruits and Health

学习关于夏季水果的简单英语。

Summer is hot. Many people like to eat fruits. Watermelon, mango, and pineapple are popular. They are sweet and cool. You can eat them every day.

夏天很热。很多人喜欢吃水果。西瓜、芒果和菠萝很受欢迎。它们又甜又凉。你可以每天吃它们。

Fruits are good for your body. They have vitamins and water. Eating fruits helps you stay healthy. You feel fresh after eating them.

水果对你的身体好。它们有维生素和水。吃水果帮助你保持健康。吃后你会感觉清爽。

You can make fruit salad or fruit juice. It is easy and fun. Share fruits with your family. Summer is a great time to enjoy fruits.

你可以做水果沙拉或果汁。这很简单又有趣。和家人分享水果。夏天是享受水果的好时机。
