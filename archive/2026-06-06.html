World Environment Day

Date: 2026-06-06 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Environmental protection

今天我们来学习关于世界环境日的一些简单英语。

World Environment Day is celebrated every year on June 5. This special day reminds us to take care of our beautiful planet. We can do simple things like planting trees or picking up trash. Even small actions can help the Earth stay healthy and clean. Let&#39;s learn how we can protect our environment today and every day.

世界环境日在每年6月5日庆祝。这个特殊的日子提醒我们关爱我们美丽的星球。我们可以做一些简单的事情，比如种树或捡垃圾。即使小小的行动也能帮助地球保持健康干净。让我们学习如何今天和每一天保护环境。

Many countries hold events for this day. People join in activities such as cycling instead of driving cars. They try to use less plastic and recycle more. These small changes in our daily life can make a big difference for the environment. It is important to remember that our actions have an impact.

许多国家为这一天举办活动。人们参加像骑自行车代替开车这样的活动。他们尽量少用塑料并多回收。我们日常生活中的这些小改变能为环境带来大不同。重要的是要记住，我们的行动会产生影响。

You don&#39;t need to go far to help. You can start at home. For example, turn off lights when you leave a room. Reuse shopping bags and water bottles. Also, save water by taking shorter showers. Every little step counts and together we can make the world a better place. Start with one change and see how good it feels.

你不需要走远来帮忙。你可以从家里开始。例如，离开房间时关灯。重复使用购物袋和水瓶。另外，通过缩短淋浴时间来节约用水。每一小步都很重要，团结起来我们能让世界更美好。从一个改变开始，看看感觉有多好。
