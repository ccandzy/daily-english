Mastering the Cornell Note-Taking System

Date: 2025-03-30 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Study Tips: Note-Taking

康奈尔笔记法帮助你将笔记分成主观点、细节和摘要，非常适合考前复习。

The Cornell note-taking system is a popular and effective method for organizing information. It divides a standard page into three clear sections: a narrow left column for main ideas or cues, a wide right column for detailed notes, and a bottom summary area. This simple structure helps you review key points quickly and efficiently. It encourages active listening and reduces the need to rewrite notes later.

康奈尔笔记系统是一种流行且有效的信息整理方法。它将标准页面分为三个清晰的部分：左侧窄栏记录主要观点或提示，右侧宽栏记录详细笔记，底部为总结区域。这种简单的结构能帮助你快速高效地回顾要点。它鼓励主动倾听，并减少了以后重写笔记的需要。

During a lecture or while reading a textbook, write down the main ideas in the left column. In the right column, jot down supporting details, examples, and your own questions. After the session, write a short summary at the bottom using your own words to reinforce learning. It also helps you identify gaps in your understanding.

听课或阅读课本时，在左侧栏写下主要观点。在右侧栏记下支撑细节、例子和你自己的问题。课程结束后，用你自己的话在底部写一个简短总结以巩固学习。这还能帮助你发现理解中的漏洞。

This method not only saves time but also improves comprehension and long-term recall. By reviewing your summaries regularly, you can prepare for exams more effectively. Try using symbols, abbreviations, and diagrams to speed up note-taking and make it more visual. Many students find that practicing the Cornell method leads to better grades and deeper learning.

这种方法不仅节省时间，还能提高理解和长期记忆。通过定期复习你的总结，你可以更有效地准备考试。尝试使用符号、缩写和图表来加快记笔记的速度，并使其更具视觉性。许多学生发现练习康奈尔笔记法能带来更好的成绩和更深层次的学习。
