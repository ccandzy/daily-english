Summer Fun: Staying Cool

Date: 2026-06-24 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Summer safety and activities

夏天如何保持凉爽和安全？

Summer is very hot. We need to stay cool. Drink a lot of water every day. Wear a hat and sunglasses when you go outside. Swimming is a good way to cool down and have fun.

夏天很热。我们需要保持凉爽。每天喝大量的水。外出时戴帽子和太阳镜。游泳是降温和玩乐的好方法。

Use sunscreen before you go out. The sun can hurt your skin. Stay in the shade when it is too hot. Eat cold fruits like watermelon and ice cream. But do not eat too much ice cream!

外出前使用防晒霜。阳光会伤害你的皮肤。天太热时待在阴凉处。吃冷水果，比如西瓜和冰淇淋。但不要吃太多冰淇淋！

Summer is a fun season. You can play outside with your friends. Go to the park or the beach. Enjoy the sun, but be careful. Remember to drink water and rest. Have a great summer!

夏天是一个有趣的季节。你可以和朋友在外面玩。去公园或海滩。享受阳光，但要小心。记得喝水和休息。祝你夏天愉快！
