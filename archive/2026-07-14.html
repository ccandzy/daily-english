E-Bikes: A Quiet Revolution

Date: 2025-07-14 | Level: Beginner | Topic: The growing popularity of electric bicycles in cities

阅读这篇关于电动自行车日益流行的文章，学习新词汇和短语。

Electric bicycles, or e-bikes, are becoming very popular in cities around the world. They are a great way to travel without using much energy. Many people use them for commuting to work or school because they are faster than walking and cheaper than a car. Also, they are better for the environment because they produce no pollution.

电动自行车（或称电单车）在世界各地的城市中越来越受欢迎。它们是一种不需要花费太多力气就能出行的好方式。许多人用它们通勤上班或上学，因为它们比步行快，比汽车便宜。而且，它们对环境更好，因为不产生污染。

The e-bike market has grown a lot over the past few years. Companies are making better batteries that last longer. Some e-bikes can go up to 25 kilometers per hour. Many cities are building special roads for bikes to keep riders safe. However, there are also some concerns. For example, e-bikes can be heavy and expensive to repair.

电动自行车市场在过去几年里增长了很多。公司正在制造续航更久的电池。有些电动自行车时速可达25公里。许多城市正在建设专门的自行车道以保证骑行者安全。然而，也存在一些担忧。例如，电动自行车可能很重，维修费用也高。

If you are thinking about getting an e-bike, you have many choices. There are different styles for different needs. Some are small and foldable for easy storage. Others are strong for long trips. Remember to wear a helmet and follow the traffic rules. E-bikes are fun and useful, but safety always comes first.

如果你在考虑购买一辆电动自行车，你有很多选择。不同的款式满足不同的需求。有些小巧可折叠，便于存放；其他则结实耐用，适合长途旅行。记得戴头盔并遵守交通规则。电动自行车既有趣又实用，但安全永远是第一位的。
