世界环境日

Date: 2026-06-08 | Level: Beginner | Topic: 保护我们的地球

学习如何用英语谈论环境保护。

World Environment Day is on June 5 every year. It is a special day to think about our planet. Many people do fun activities like planting trees or cleaning parks. These activities help keep the Earth clean and beautiful. We can all do something nice for the Earth. Everyone can join and enjoy this day.

世界环境日在每年的6月5日。这是一个思考我们星球的特殊日子。许多人做有趣的活动，比如植树或清理公园。这些活动帮助保持地球干净美丽。我们都可以为地球做些好事。每个人都可以参与并享受这一天。

This year, the main idea is plastic pollution. Plastic is not good for animals and the ocean. It stays in the environment for a very long time. We can use fewer plastic bags and bottles. For example, bring your own bag when shopping. Also, use a water bottle many times. Let&#39;s learn how to protect our environment.

今年，主题是塑料污染。塑料对动物和海洋不好。它在环境中停留很长时间。我们可以少用塑料袋和瓶子。例如，购物时自带袋子。此外，多次使用水瓶。让我们学习如何保护环境。

Little changes can help a lot. Turn off lights when you leave a room. Walk or ride a bike instead of using a car. Also, recycle paper, cans, and glass. Every small action makes a big difference for our world. Remember, every bit counts! Start doing these things today.

小改变能帮助很多。离开房间时关灯。走路或骑自行车代替开车。此外，回收纸张、罐子和玻璃。每一个小行动都为我们的世界带来大不同。记住，每一点都重要！从今天开始做这些事。
