The Rise of Electric Bicycles in Cities

Date: 2026-07-17 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Urban transportation

本文讨论电动自行车在城市中日益流行的原因及其好处。注意新词汇和短语。

Electric bicycles, or e-bikes, have become very popular in many cities around the world. They offer a clean way to travel short distances. More people choose them because they are fast, cheap, and good for the environment. Many cities build special lanes for cyclists.

电动自行车，或称e-bike，在世界许多城市变得非常流行。它们提供了一种清洁的短途出行方式。越来越多的人选择它们，因为它们快速、便宜且环保。许多城市为骑行者修建了专用车道。

Experts say that e-bikes help reduce traffic and air pollution. They are helpful for commuters who want to avoid crowded buses or trains. With a battery, the bike can go faster without much effort. This makes it easier for older people and those who are not very fit.

专家表示，电动自行车有助于减少交通拥堵和空气污染。它们对想要避开拥挤公交或地铁的通勤者很有帮助。有了电池，自行车可以更省力地加速。这让老年人和不太健壮的人更容易骑行。

However, there are some safety concerns. Accidents sometimes happen when e-bikes go too fast. It is important to wear a helmet and follow traffic rules. Governments are making new laws to keep everyone safe. The future of e-bikes looks bright as technology improves.

然而，也存在一些安全问题。当电动自行车速度过快时，有时会发生事故。佩戴头盔并遵守交通规则很重要。政府正在制定新法律以保障所有人的安全。随着技术的进步，电动自行车的未来看起来一片光明。
