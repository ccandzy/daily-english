Electric Cars for Beginners

Date: 2026-06-09 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Electric cars

本课介绍电动汽车的基本知识，帮助初学者学习简单英语。

Today, many people use electric cars. They are very popular in big cities. Electric cars do not use gasoline. They run on electricity from batteries. You can charge a car at home or at a charging station. The car is quiet and clean.

如今，许多人使用电动汽车。它们在大城市非常受欢迎。电动汽车不使用汽油。它们靠电池的电能运行。你可以在家或充电站为汽车充电。这种车安静又清洁。

Electric cars are good for the earth. They make less air pollution. They also cost less to drive. You do not need to buy gasoline. Just plug in the car. Many drivers say they enjoy driving electric cars.

电动汽车对地球有益。它们减少空气污染。驾驶成本也更低。你不需要购买汽油。只需插上电源。许多司机说他们喜欢开电动汽车。

The future of cars is electric. Many car companies are making new electric models. Governments give money to people who buy electric cars. Soon, we will see more electric cars on the road.

汽车的未来是电动的。许多汽车公司正在制造新的电动车型。政府为购买电动汽车的人提供补贴。很快，我们将看到路上有更多电动汽车。
