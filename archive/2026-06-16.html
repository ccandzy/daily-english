World Health Day 2025

Date: 2025-04-07 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Health and Wellness

今天是世界卫生日，我们学习一些关于健康的简单英语。

World Health Day is on April 7 every year. This year, the theme is about healthy living. We can learn simple ways to be healthy. First, eat a balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, and grains. Second, drink at least eight glasses of water a day. Third, avoid smoking and too much alcohol. Remember, small changes can make a big difference.

世界卫生日在每年的4月7日。今年的主题是关于健康生活。我们可以学习简单的保持健康的方法。首先，吃均衡的饮食，包括水果、蔬菜和谷物。其次，每天至少喝八杯水。第三，避免吸烟和过量饮酒。记住，小的改变可以带来大的不同。

Physical activity is important for everyone. You don&#39;t need to go to the gym. You can walk, run, ride a bike, or even dance at home. Try to do at least 30 minutes of exercise each day. It keeps your heart strong and your mind clear. You will feel more energetic. Choose an activity you enjoy so you will stick with it.

体育活动对每个人都很重要。你不需要去健身房。你可以走路、跑步、骑自行车，甚至在家里跳舞。尽量每天做至少30分钟的运动。它能让你的心脏强壮，头脑清晰。你会感觉更有活力。选择一项你喜欢的活动，这样你就能坚持下去。

Sleep is just as important as diet and exercise. Most adults need 7 to 9 hours of sleep per night. Good sleep helps you remember things and stay focused. It also boosts your immune system. To sleep better, keep a regular schedule and avoid screens before bed. A peaceful bedroom environment helps too.

睡眠和饮食、运动一样重要。大多数成年人每晚需要7到9小时的睡眠。良好的睡眠有助于你记住事情并保持专注。它还能增强你的免疫系统。为了更好地睡眠，保持规律的作息，睡前避免看屏幕。安静舒适的卧室环境也有帮助。
