Planting Trees for a Greener City

Date: 2026-06-07 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Tree Planting

本文介绍城市种树的好处，以及如何参与种树活动。

Many cities around the world are planting more trees. For example, in China, the government encourages people to plant trees every spring. Trees make the air clean and the city beautiful. People enjoy walking in parks with many trees. Trees also help reduce noise.

世界上许多城市正在种植更多的树。例如，在中国，政府鼓励人们每年春天种树。树木让空气清新，让城市美丽。人们喜欢在有很多树的公园里散步。树木还能帮助减少噪音。

Volunteers often help plant trees in public places like parks and streets. They dig holes and put young trees into the ground. Then they water them. It is hard work but very rewarding. Everyone feels happy to see trees grow and provide shade for hot days.

志愿者经常在公园和街道等公共场所帮忙种树。他们挖洞，把树苗放入土中，然后浇水。这是一项辛苦但非常有意义的工作。看到树木生长并在炎热的日子里提供阴凉，大家都感到开心。

We can also plant small trees in our garden or balcony at home. It is easy and fun to do with family. Trees give us fresh air, shade, oxygen, and even fruits. Planting trees is good for the environment and our health. Let&#39;s all take part and make our world greener!

我们也可以在家里的花园或阳台上种小树。和家人一起做既简单又有趣。树木给我们提供新鲜空气、阴凉、氧气，甚至水果。种树对环境和健康都有益。让我们一起参与，让世界更绿色吧！
