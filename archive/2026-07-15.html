Smartphones and Sleep

Date: 2026-07-15 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Health & Technology

睡前少用手机，睡眠会更好。

Many people check their smartphones just before they go to sleep. They read messages, watch short videos, or scroll through social media. However, this habit can make it harder to fall asleep and reduce sleep quality.

很多人在睡觉前会看手机。他们读信息、看短视频或刷社交媒体。然而，这个习惯会让人更难入睡，也会降低睡眠质量。

The screen of a smartphone sends out blue light. This blue light tells your brain that it is still daytime, so your brain stops producing melatonin, a chemical that helps you sleep. Experts suggest putting away your phone at least 30 minutes before bed.

手机的屏幕会发出蓝光。这种蓝光会告诉你的大脑现在还是白天，所以大脑会停止分泌褪黑素——一种帮助睡眠的化学物质。专家建议至少在睡前30分钟放下手机。

Instead of using your phone at night, you can try other relaxing activities. Reading a book, listening to soft music, or doing some light stretching may help you fall asleep more easily. A good night&#39;s sleep will make you feel more energetic the next day.

晚上不用手机的话，你可以尝试其他放松的活动。看书、听轻柔的音乐或做些轻微的拉伸运动，可能让你更容易入睡。一夜好眠会让你第二天更有精神。
