World Music Day

Date: 2026-06-21 | Level: Beginner | Topic: World Music Day on June 21

今天我们来学习关于世界音乐日的简单英语。记住，音乐让我们开心！

World Music Day is a special day that celebrates music around the world. It takes place every year on June 21. On this day, people from many countries play music, sing songs, and dance. Music is a fun activity for everyone, young and old. It is a time to share joy and creativity. Many communities organize events to bring people together.

世界音乐日是庆祝世界各地音乐的特殊日子。每年6月21日举行。这一天，来自许多国家的人们演奏音乐、唱歌和跳舞。音乐对每个人来说都是一项有趣的活动，无论老少。这是一个分享快乐和创造力的时刻。许多社区组织活动将人们聚集在一起。

There are many free concerts and events in parks and streets. You can listen to different music styles, such as classical, jazz, pop, and rock. Some people perform with guitars, pianos, or drums. Others sing or dance in groups. Music can make us feel happy, calm, or energetic. It is a universal language that everyone can understand. You do not need to know the words to enjoy a melody.

公园和街道上有很多免费的音乐会和活动。你可以听到不同的音乐风格，如古典、爵士、流行和摇滚。有些人用吉他、钢琴或鼓表演。其他人则分组唱歌或跳舞。音乐可以让我们感到开心、平静或充满活力。它是一种每个人都能理解的通用语言。你不需要知道歌词就能享受旋律。

Music is good for our mind and body. It helps us relax after a long day. It also brings people together, even if they speak different languages. On World Music Day, everyone can enjoy the magic of music. You can listen to your favorite songs or discover new ones. Music connects us all. So turn on some music and celebrate!

音乐对我们的身心都有好处。它帮助我们在一整天后放松。它也将人们聚集在一起，即使他们说着不同的语言。在世界音乐日，每个人都可以享受音乐的魔力。你可以听你最喜欢的歌曲或发现新歌。音乐将我们所有人连接起来。所以，打开音乐，庆祝吧！
