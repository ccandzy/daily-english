The Rise of Electric Bikes

Date: 2026-07-06 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Electric bikes (e-bikes) are becoming a popular form of transportation in many cities.

本课程介绍电动自行车的流行趋势及其优缺点。

Electric bikes, or e-bikes, are becoming very popular in many cities around the world. With a small motor and a battery, e-bikes help riders travel longer distances without getting too tired. Many people use them to commute to work or school because they are faster than regular bikes and cheaper than cars.

电动自行车，简称电单车，在世界许多城市越来越受欢迎。凭借小型电机和电池，电单车帮助骑行者走更远的路而不会太累。很多人用它通勤上班或上学，因为它比普通自行车快，比汽车便宜。

One big advantage of e-bikes is that they are environmentally friendly. They use electricity instead of gasoline, which helps reduce air pollution. Also, they are a great way to exercise without too much effort. You can choose how much help you want from the motor, so you can make your ride as easy or as active as you like.

电单车的一大优点是环保。它用电而不是汽油，有助于减少空气污染。此外，它还是一种不用太费力就能锻炼的好方法。你可以选择电机提供多少帮助，从而让骑行变得轻松或活跃。

However, e-bikes also bring some challenges. Because they can go faster than normal bikes, safety is very important. Riders should always wear a helmet and follow traffic rules. Many cities have new regulations about where and how fast you can ride an e-bike. As the number of e-bikes grows, it is important for everyone to learn how to use them safely.

然而，电单车也带来一些挑战。因为它们比普通自行车快，安全非常重要。骑车者应始终戴头盔并遵守交通规则。许多城市对电单车的骑行地点和速度出台了新规定。随着电单车数量的增加，每个人都学会安全使用它们非常重要。
