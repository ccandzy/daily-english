Learn about International Olympic Day

Date: 2026-06-23 | Level: Beginner | Topic: International Olympic Day

本课介绍国际奥林匹克日，学习简单词汇。

International Olympic Day is celebrated every year on June 23. It is a day to enjoy sports and the Olympic spirit. The Olympic spirit means friendship, respect, and fair play. People from many countries join fun activities. They run, jump, swim, and play team games. This day reminds us that sports are for everyone, not just athletes. Schools and clubs often hold special events.

国际奥林匹克日是每年的6月23日。这是一个享受运动和奥林匹克精神的日子。奥林匹克精神意味着友谊、尊重和公平竞争。来自许多国家的人们参加有趣的活动。他们跑步、跳跃、游泳和玩团队游戏。这一天提醒我们，运动不仅仅属于运动员，而是属于每个人。学校和俱乐部经常举办特别活动。

The first Olympic Games were in ancient Greece over 2,000 years ago. Only men could take part. Today, the Olympics are for all people. Athletes from over 200 countries compete. They show great skill and determination. The Olympic Games help bring the world together. They promote peace and understanding. The five rings on the Olympic flag represent the five continents.

第一届奥林匹克运动会始于两千多年前的古希腊。当时只有男性可以参加。如今，奥运会面向所有人。来自200多个国家的运动员同场竞技。他们展示了高超的技巧和决心。奥运会帮助世界团结在一起，促进和平与理解。奥运会会旗上的五环代表五大洲。

On International Olympic Day, you can try a new sport or activity. You can play basketball, ride a bike, or do yoga. Exercise is good for your body and mind. You can also watch Olympic videos to see incredible moments. Remember, sports are about having fun and staying healthy. Everyone can be an athlete in their own way. So get moving and enjoy!

在国际奥林匹克日，你可以尝试一项新的运动或活动。你可以打篮球、骑自行车或做瑜伽。锻炼对你的身心都有好处。你也可以观看奥运视频，欣赏令人惊叹的瞬间。记住，运动是为了乐趣和健康。每个人都可以以自己的方式成为运动员。所以，动起来，享受吧！
