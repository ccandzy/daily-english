How to Stay Healthy in Summer

Date: 2026-06-04 | Level: Beginner | Topic: Health tips for the summer season

夏季健康小贴士：多喝水，多吃水果，适度运动，保证睡眠。

Summer is very hot. You need to take care of your health. Drink a lot of water every day. Water helps your body stay cool. Eat fresh fruits like watermelon and oranges. They have vitamins and are good for you.

夏天很热。你需要照顾好自己的健康。每天喝很多水。水帮助你的身体保持凉爽。吃新鲜水果，比如西瓜和橙子。它们含有维生素，对你有好处。

Do not stay in the sun too long. Use an umbrella or wear a hat. Light clothes are better. Also, do some exercise in the morning or evening. It is not too hot then. Walking or swimming is good for your body.

不要在太阳下待太久。用伞或戴帽子。穿浅色衣服更好。另外，在早上或晚上做一些运动。那时不太热。散步或游泳对你的身体有好处。

Get enough sleep at night. Sleep helps you feel good. Eat light food like salads and vegetables. Do not eat too much heavy food. Follow these tips, and you will have a happy and healthy summer.

晚上要睡够。睡眠帮助你感觉良好。吃清淡的食物，比如沙拉和蔬菜。不要吃太多油腻的食物。遵循这些建议，你就会有一个快乐健康的夏天。
